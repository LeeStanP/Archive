Redwood Reporting

The Redwood Reporting software delivers clarity by automatically generating and distributing critical reports precisely when, where, and how you need them. Our advanced report automation tools eliminate hassle, saving time, and reduce errors by streamlining repetitive tasks and seamlessly integrating data from disparate systems and sources.

Automated Report Management, Distribution and Archiving

Redwood Reporting combines automation with governance, ensuring critical reports are generated, distributed, and archived under strict compliance protocols. Audit trails, role-based access, and tamper-proof logging let you trust every number—while our automation eliminates the errors of manual handling.

Keep Things Moving

Easily inspect and evaluate documents with the Redwood Reporting Approver. Add user feedback before moving items to the next step in the approval process for improved data accuracy.

Understand and Deliver

Analyze document content in text, PDF or Excel formats, then deliver specific reports that adhere to business rules with the Redwood Reporting Burster.

Deliver Custom Results

Combine data from multiple, disparate sources to produce personalized, unified reports for distribution via email or shared publication with the Redwood Reporting Bundler.
