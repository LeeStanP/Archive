Smart Card

We lead in advanced payment technologies, securing EMV transactions and creating innovative payment solutions for forward-thinking banks & FinTechs.

IDEMIA’s payment card technologies are securing EMV transactions worldwide. Combining the strength of a global leader with the flexibility of a local partner, we collaborate with both domestic and international schemes. Through continuous investment in cutting-edge payment card technologies, we deliver the highest level of security while creating unique, personalized experiences for cardholders. 

Motion Code

IDEMIA is full of innovative minds. And they have developed a seamless way to drastically enhance the security of Card-Not-Present (CNP) transactions. MOTION CODE cardholders will find that the traditional static 3-digit security code on the back of their card has been replaced by a mini-screen displaying a code, which automatically refreshes every hour.

Metal Card

From Mass Affluent to High Net Worth segmentations, the banking market is looking for strong distinctive products and exclusive services to win and retain high-value cardholders. With its portfolio of high-end metal payment cards, IDEMIA proposes a large range of attractive and eye-catching metal card bodies.

IDEMIA Card Connect

Bridges the digital and physical worlds, streamlining the banking experience from card activation to Secure Customer Authentication (SCA). This versatile solution opens endless possibilities, enabling banks to create innovative use cases that make everyday banking simpler and more convenient for customers.

Illuminating Payment Cards

The IDEMIA Starlight card features an OLED panel and operates without a battery. Using advanced OLED technology, the card illuminates a unique pattern when placed near a POS reader, powered entirely by the NFC field.

GREENPAY Sustainable Payment Solutions

GREENPAY reflects IDEMIA’s dedication to transforming how plastic is designed, used, and reused in payments. It offers customers innovative, eco-friendly solutions, paving the way for a more sustainable future.
