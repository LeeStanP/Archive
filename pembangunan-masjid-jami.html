Construction of the Jami Nur Sholihah Mosque

PT. Integra Pratama, based in West Jakarta, is dedicated to supporting the communities in which we operate. As part of this commitment, we took the initiative to assist with the ongoing construction of the Jami Nur Sholihah Mosque. Located on Jl. Panjang Kp. Baru, RT 008/06, South Sukabumi, Kec. Kebon Jeruk, West Jakarta, the mosque also serves as a place of study for around 30 school-age orphans. To support the mosque and its young students, we provided direct cash assistance, as well as basic food packages and financial aid for their educational needs. This CSR activity took place on November 27, 2022, and was attended by the director and staff members of PT. Integra Pratama, the mosque’s foundation administrators, and the foster children.
