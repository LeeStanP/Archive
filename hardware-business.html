Entrust

nShield Hardware Security Modules (HSMs) provide a hardened, tamper-resistant environment for secure cryptographic processing, key generation and protection, encryption, HSM key management, and more.

nShield General Purpose HSMs

nShield General Purpose HSMs provide a hardened, high-assurance platform for cryptographic operations, including key generation, storage, and processing. Supporting industry standards such as FIPS 140-2/3 and PCI DSS, these modules enable enterprises to meet stringent security requirements while integrating seamlessly with existing infrastructure—from financial services and healthcare to government and IoT ecosystems.

The nShield Difference: Security World

Only nShield HSMs feature Security World, a specialized architecture that provides unmatched capabilities in several crucial areas:

  • Full control: Security World provides strong, granular controls over the access and usage of keys and policies.
  • Easier backups: Unlike alternatives that require backup HSMs and labor-intensive HSM cloning, Security World enables simple, automated backups of HSM files.
  • Scalability and flexibility: Security World delivers unlimited key storage and the flexibility needed to align your HSM operations with your organization’s specific needs.
  • Streamlined HSM operations: nShield HSMs can be managed centrally based on unified policy and operational administration, no matter how many nShields you deploy.

NSHIELD SOLO

PCIe Cards for Embedding in Appliances or Servers

nShield Solo HSMs are low-profile PCI-Express card modules that deliver cryptographic services to applications hosted on a server or appliance. nShield Solo HSMs are available in two series: classic nShield Solo+ HSMs and the high-performance nShield Solo XC HSM series.

NSHIELD AS A SERVICE

nShield as a Service provides access to dedicated FIPS 140-2 Level 3 certified nShield Connect XC HSMs via a subscription model. The solution delivers the same features and functionality as on-premises HSMs combined with the benefits of a cloud service deployment. This allows customers to fulfill their cloud first objectives and leave the maintenance of these appliances to the experts at nCipher. Available as Self Managed and Fully Managed service options.
